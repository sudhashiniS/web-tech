S. P. Balasubrahmanyam

S.P. Balasubrahmanyam was born on June 4, 1946 in Konetampet, Pallipattu, Chittoor District, Andhra Pradesh [now Tiruvallur District, Tamil Nadu], India. He was a music artist and actor, known for Maine Pyar Kiya (1989), Enthiran (2010) and Andaz Apna Apna (1994). He was married to Savitri S.. He died on September 25, 2020 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India.
